Our Safety Standards

Your safety is our top priority. We ensure that every ride you take with us meets the highest standards of safety and security.

  • Driver Background Checks: All our drivers undergo thorough background checks and are vetted for criminal history and driving records.
  • Vehicle Inspections: Our vehicles are regularly inspected to ensure they meet safety and maintenance standards.
  • In-Car Safety Features: All cars are equipped with safety features such as airbags, ABS, and GPS tracking to ensure a secure ride.

COVID-19 Safety and Hygiene Protocols

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have implemented rigorous safety and hygiene protocols. These measures are activated only if the UK is in an emergency COVID-19 state, ensuring your safety during such times.

  • Regular Sanitization: When activated, vehicles are sanitized before and after every ride, with special attention to high-touch areas like door handles, seat belts, and seats.
  • Driver Health Checks: Drivers undergo daily health screenings and are required to wear masks at all times during rides in emergency states.
  • Hand Sanitizers: Hand sanitizers are made available in all vehicles for passengers during these periods.
  • Contactless Payment: We encourage contactless payments to minimize physical interaction when these protocols are in effect.

Please note that these protocols are dynamic and will be reinstated as necessary to align with government guidelines during an emergency COVID-19 situation.

Booking Terms

Refund will not be given to passengers who do not wait for their allocated driver and use alternative transport facility. Please make sure that all your pick up dates and times are in UK time zone and not of the departure airports. If your flight has any serious delays please inform us or the driver as soon as possible. The driver is within his rights to ask for advance payments for cash journeys exceeding 10 miles if he feels it necessary to do so. If the information provided by the customer during online booking form is not enough then every effort will be made to contact the customer to acquire missing information. If we can’t make contact with the customer for clarification, then any such bookings will remain unconfirmed.

In-Cab Card Terminal Charges

Minimum card payment is £10 . There are no extra transaction charges for paying in the cab with your card, as long as the card is either registered in UK or EEA European Economic Area. All Non European Cards and Corporate Cards will incur a surcharge of 2.5%. Cancellation Policy/Cancellation charges You can cancel your bookings at anytime, however charges will apply as follows. Cancellation before 48 hours to the pickup time - Full Refund Cancellations between 12 to 48 hours before pickup time - admin charges of 20% will apply. Cancellations within 12 hours to pick up time or afterwards – No Refunds All cancellations should be made via email only. If the passenger doesn’t show up for whatever reason, no refund will be issued. By booking with us, you agree to our terms and conditions.
